About us

Our Mission

To create opportunities for African youth by training them on digital, software and technological skills. This prepares them for the future of work by equipping them with marketable, industry driven skills.

Our goal is to ensure that our talented youth are available and trained in order to meet the growing opportunities and needs of the ICT Industry in Africa.

We are nationally recognized for providing a state-of-the-art learning environment in a wide spectrum of applied technology fields. Our programs include University Boot Camps, High Schools Trainings, Corporate Trainings, Mobile Software Development Programs, Data Science, IBM Certifications, Cyber Security and USSD.

We also envision an explosion of technology innovation and the creation of successful tech ventures by local developers from across the country with a zeal for changing the world through harnessing the power of their technology training for social good.
